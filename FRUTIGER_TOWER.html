If you're into parkour, check out "Frutiger Aero Tower!" It's a chill platformer themed Frutiger in 3 different styles: Metro, Frutiger, and Punk (?). Complete all 3 towers, and buy merch in the lobby store!

This is a really well-made game, I found myself admiring the scenery on the way up. The parkour is really well-designed and creative, using elements from the theme pretty well and overall, just being a 10/10 experience.
