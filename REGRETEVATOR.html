"Regretevator" is an elevator-style Roblox game where you can go from daunting boss battles, to simple minigames! Rack up floors, coins, and friends as you embark on this elevator adventure!

I love this game for the attention to detail in structures and gameplay, the characters and their interactions, the newly-established daily challenges, and all the different ways there are to explore the game. Whether you're a pro or a casual player, there's always going to be a way for you to enjoy it!! (Though be warned, you will die a LOT at the start)
