Test your cooking skills in "Generic Cooking Game!" With somethig for all players both seasoned and new, find your way around the kitchen, serve customers, and establish some cooking basics!

I first got into this game about a year ago, but couldn't take the heat... so I got out of the kitchen (iykyk). Exploring this game a year later, it's actually pretty fun! You can start off in a solo kitchen to level up, then transfer to a public server to be in a chill environment where you cook with other chefs. They're susceptible to griefing at times, so if you want to play in a more serious environment I recommend playing solo until level 15, where you can play ranked.
